## Review Strategy

Online reviews are critical for local rankings and conversion. Implement this process:

1. After each student placement, send a WhatsApp message asking for a Google Review
2. Create a short link to your Google Review page and share it in all student WhatsApp groups
3. Respond to every review — positive and negative — professionally
4. Do NOT offer incentives for reviews (violates Google policy)
5. Target: 4.5+ rating with 50+ reviews within 6 months
